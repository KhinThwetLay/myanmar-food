About Myanmar Food

ミャンマー料理は、ハーブやスパイス、発酵食品を使った、優しくも奥深い味わいが特徴です。油を巧みに使った煮込み料理や、食感豊かなサラダ、そしてココナッツミルクを使った甘いデザートまで、多彩な食の体験があなたを待っています。

It's known for its savory salads (thoke), rich curries, and noodle soups. The food is not just about taste but also about texture, with a common inclusion of crispy, crunchy elements.

Top Myanmar Dishes

Mohinga

  • What is it? Rice noodle fish soup — considered Myanmar’s national dish.
  • Main Ingredients: Catfish broth, banana stem, rice noodles, garlic, lemongrass, boiled egg.
  • Why try it? Savory, rich, and perfect for breakfast.

Laphet Thoke

  • What is it? Fermented tea leaf salad.
  • Main Ingredients: Pickled tea leaves, fried beans, nuts, garlic, tomatoes, lime juice.
  • Why try it? A unique mix of sour, bitter, crunchy, and spicy.

Shan Noodles

  • What is it? A dry noodle dish from Shan State.
  • Main Ingredients: Rice noodles, chicken or pork, garlic oil, pickled veggies.
  • Why try it? Mild and flavorful — great comfort food.

Ohn No Khao Swe

  • What is it? Coconut chicken noodle soup.
  • Main Ingredients: Coconut milk, chicken, boiled egg, turmeric, chickpea flour.
  • Why try it? Creamy and similar to Thai Khao Soi.

Ngapi Yay Dipping Sauce

  • What is it? Fermented fish paste dip.
  • Served with: Fresh vegetables like cabbage, okra, cucumber.
  • Why try it? It’s salty, spicy, and very traditional.
